Travel + Acommodations

Venue

The ceremony and dinner will be held outside, on the lawn of the Sesnon House. It will likely be warm, but it might be windy or overcast, so we recommend bringing layers as well as sunscreen, just in case.

Location

The Sesnon House is located on the Cabrillo College campus in Aptos, 7 miles south of Santa Cruz and 77 miles south of San Francisco (see map below for driving directions). San Francisco Airport is 65 miles away, San Jose Airport is 38 miles north and, Oakland Airport is 74 miles north.

Airports and Shuttles

The Santa Cruz Airport Flyer travels from both San Jose and San Francisco airports to the Best Western Seacliff Inn in Aptos (see below for hotel details). Call (831) 423-5937 or visit their website here for more information.
